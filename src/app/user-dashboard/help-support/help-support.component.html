Help and Support

If you have any questions or concerns about our food donation website, please feel free to contact us using the form on our contact page.

FAQs

  • How can I donate food?

    To donate food, please go to our donate page and fill out the form. We will then contact you to arrange for pickup or dropoff of the food.

  • What types of food do you accept?

    We accept all types of non-perishable food items, as well as perishable items that can be stored safely (such as fresh produce).

  • How do you distribute the donated food?

    We work with local food banks and charities to distribute the donated food to those in need.

  • How can I get involved with your organization?

    We are always looking for volunteers and donations to help support our mission. Please visit our Home > about page to learn more.
